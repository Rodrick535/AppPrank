Disabled apps prank

This site is part of what will allow you disable your friend's apps.

How to do it:

  1. Take a screenshot of your friend's home screen with the app visible. This can be done by
    pressing the power button and the home button at the same time.
  2. Go to iempty.tooliphone.net
  3. Select "Create BLANK icons"
  4. Upload the screenshot
  5. There should be a grid of apps. Click the one you want to disable.
  6. Click "Launch custom app"
  7. Set the app type to URL. From there, type the link https://rodrick535.github.io/AppPrank/disabled.html
  8. Click "Done", then follow the prompts.
  9. When naming the bookmark, replace the "?" with whatever the app name is.
  10. Hide the actual app in a folder somewhere
  11. You're done. Now watch your friend's reaction to having their app disabled.
